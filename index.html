WATER RING TOSS GAME

Instructions:

  • 1. Click the "Move Rings" button to start the movement of the rings.
  • 2. Click the "Stop" button to stop the movement of the rings.
  • 3. Try to get the rings into the target by using the buttons.
  • 4. Each ring that lands in the target earns you 100 points.
  • 5. If a ring hits the canvas walls, you lose 50 points.
  • 6. If your score drops below -1000, the game is over. You'll see a message indicating you lost.
  • 7. If you collect all the rings, the game will stop automatically, and your score will be displayed.
  • 8. Your high score will be saved and displayed.
